What's On

Ethos Session
Every Thursday
6-8pm
Come along to Ethos every Thursday of term-time for super-cheap top rope, lead climbing and bouldering. £2 for Club members, £4 for non-members.
RavensWall Session
Alternate Mondays
6-8pm
We get discounted entry to RavensWall (and other LCC centres) for £8.50 - £7 off full price with shoe hire. The deal applies at all off-peak times but we go as a Club every other week.
Westway Session
Alternate Mondays
6-8pm
When we don't go to RavensWall, we go to Westway on Mondays. With over 150 rope lines, it is a great place to get some proper top-rope and lead climbing done, with some bouldering too.
Snowdonia Trip
20-22 Jan
Meet 4:30pm
We're heading back to Snowdonia for the first trip this term. Join us for lots of climbing and hiking, and possibly som,e trail running too.
